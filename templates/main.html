When you want your money to grow, you can do something called investing. That means spending some of your money to buy little pieces of a company. This helps the company, because when people buy these little pieces, called shares, it gives the company the money it needs to keep growing.

To say thank you, the companies give you some of the money they earn later! There are lots interesting and different ways to invest.

One is stock, which buys shares from just one company. Another is a mutual fund, which buys shares from many companies in one bundle, and is handled by a manager who is trying to beat the market. The last is called an exchange-traded fund. It's made of mutual funds (among other things) and trades them back and forth over the day with others, so that it stays as stable as it can be to keep up with the market.
